Alternative Capital Solutions


Advance Your Legacy to The Next Level

If you are a family business owner or small manufacturer of industrial product solutions, you are likely thinking about how to lead the next generation of your business to compete in today's global market space. Many viable businesses are showing up empty-handed with a clear leadership succession plan. Such owners may have a vision that is under-capitalized or unachievable without additional leadership skill sets to launch them into the future.  

cSalt has a passion for identifying such viable, long-standing organizations and assisting them with advisory and capital solutions to transition to a stronger future built on the foundation of its founders. We are searching for opportunities to apply our direct experience with growing manufacturing and distribution enterprises.

Our Core Values


Our Core Values


Progressive Growth

If a business is not growing and striving toward its potential, the consequences are grave.  We believe in getting the right results for the right reasons – and we work fast to achieve them.

Continuous Improvement

Successful products and services thrive and then die.  Continuous movement toward better efficiencies are requirements for success in today’s global market space.

Family Culture

We have always worked with family and respect the cultural care needed to keep family values and relationships in tact .  Equally important, we strive to exceed the expectations of our expanded family – our customers.

Innovative Solutions

Critical thinking skills are required to stay ahead of the game by strategizing new ways to be unique and competitive.

Strong Work Ethic

A strong work ethic with the perseverance and persistence to achieve your passion is essential to personal success.

Community

Making a difference through building community is not only the right thing, it restores hope and creates a sense of purpose for all.

Partnerships and Opportunities

We partner with proven professionals who assist us with finding, vetting and connecting mutually beneficial business and investment opportunities. Our ideal opportunities are businesses that meet some of the following characteristics:

 

Ideal Opportunities

  • Closely-held, family business with less than $10,000,000 in revenue
  • Growth-oriented businesses with capital constraints
  • Manufacturer or distributor of durable industrial products
  • Existing or potential national business scope
  • International market development
  • Supply chain enhancement or improvement opportunities
  • Owner desires to retire and has no clear succession plan
